CM-ECF Information

CM/ECF is the court's Case Management and Electronic Filing System.  Electronic Filing is mandatory for the Eastern District of Oklahoma.  The Administrative Guide provides the rules and procedures for using CM/ECF.  View the "CM/ECF Training" link for information on how to set up an account and receive training.  Our event lists show every possible type of document that can be filed in CM/ECF and the instruction section contains information on filing processes.  Lastly, the CM/ECF Basic Information & Functioning tab provides information on general use.  If you have questions, support is available by telephone or email during regular business hours at:

Telephone: (918) 684-7920
Email - CM-ECFIntake_oked@oked.uscourts.gov
