Welcome

Welcome to the United States Bankruptcy Court for the District of New Jersey. Courthouses are located in Newark, Trenton, and Camden.

Court business hours: Monday - Friday | 9am - 4pm
Drop box hours: Monday - Friday | 8:30am - 6pm (Camden and Trenton) | 8:30am - 7pm (Newark)
