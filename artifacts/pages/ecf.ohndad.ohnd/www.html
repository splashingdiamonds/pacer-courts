Coronavirus (COVID-19) Guidance


Community Level Update - September 2, 2022

Effective immediately, based on the latest Community Level updates from the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) for Cuyahoga County, visitors are no longer required to wear a mask to gain entrance into the Carl B. Stokes United States Court House.

Please be prepared and bring a mask with you when you come to any courthouse in the Northern District of Ohio in the event the Community Level changes.
