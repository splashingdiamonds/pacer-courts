Information Regarding Coronavirus Disease 2019 (COVID-19) and Court Operations

Updated:Tuesday, February 22, 2022

The Warren B. Rudman U.S. Courthouse is open to the public.  All persons seeking entry to the Rudman Courthouse are required to comply with access restrictions and screening protocols.  The Clerk's Office provides in-person assistance Monday - Friday, 8:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m.

Until further notice, all hearings in the Bankruptcy Court will be accomplished through telephonic means. Questions about hearings should be directed to the Courtroom Deputy at 603-222-2644.

The United States Bankruptcy Court for the District of New Hampshire has entered a Fifteenth General Order Regarding Court Operations Under the Exigent Circumstances Created by COVID-19. 

Please see the Court's Coronavirus Disease (COVID-19) and Court Operations webpage for more information.

Christmas Closing

The Court and the Clerk's Office will be closed on Monday, December 26, 2022 to observe Christmas. The Court's CM/ECF system will be available for electronic filing and research.
