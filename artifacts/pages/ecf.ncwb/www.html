Resumption of In-Person Hearings

In accordance with the General Order entered on March 21, 2022, beginning March 22, 2022, all Charlotte hearings will be held in-person.  Charlotte hearings will be held on the second floor of the new wing of the Charles R. Jonas Federal Building.  Participants should use the front entrance and follow Court Security Officer instructions to find the appropriate second floor courtroom: 

 

    Judge Beyer – LTB Courtroom 2A

    Judge Whitley – JCW Courtroom 2B

 

All Asheville hearings will resume in-person beginning April 5, 2022, and all Statesville hearings will resume in-person beginning April 8, 2022, and all Shelby hearings will resume in-person beginning April 29, 2022.
