Invoice Processing Platform (IPP) For Vendors

Vendors are encouraged to enroll in the Invoice Processing Platform (IPP) service for EFT payment notification.  IPP enables vendors to check EFT payment status, or choose to receive emails with a simple notification of payment or detailed remittance information.  Enrollment in IPP is completely independent of registration in SAM.  Vendors enrolled in IPP receive notification by email or telephone when an EFT payment has been sent to them.  Enrollment is completed via the IPP website: http://www.ipp.gov/index.htm

Frequently Asked Questions:
