Information Regarding Coronavirus Disease (COVID-19) and Court Operations

The United States Bankruptcy Court for the Southern District of Georgia continues to closely monitor the national response to the Coronavirus Disease (COVID-19) and will be following guidance provided by the Administrative Office of the United States Courts (AO) and the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), available here.

Please note the following information regarding entry into any courthouse in the Southern District of Georgia: Chief District Judge J. Randal Hall has issued an ORDER with an updated list of restrictions on persons entering any courthouse in the Southern District of Georgia.

Please click here to view information pertaining to COVID-19 and court operations. This will be updated as information becomes available.

Welcome

Welcome to the official website for the United States Bankruptcy Court for the Southern District of Georgia. We serve 43 counties in southeast Georgia with offices in Augusta, Brunswick, Dublin, Savannah, Waycross, and Statesboro.
