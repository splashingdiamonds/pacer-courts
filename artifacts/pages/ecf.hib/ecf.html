District of Hawaii - Document Filing System

Access to documents in cases filed before December 1, 2003, and which have been closed
for more than a year may be restricted in PACER. Documents in these cases may be viewed
via the Internet only by parties in the case who have a separate CM/ECF login issued by
the United States Bankruptcy Court for the District of Hawaii. For information about viewing
these documents in the Clerk's Office or ordering copies by mail, please see the
information about Copy Requests at the court's website.

All case information is available by logging in with a PACER account and password.
