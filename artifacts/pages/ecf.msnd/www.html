Standard Maintenance Window for The Northern District of Mississippi: On Saturday mornings from 6 am - 12 pm CST the ECF (Electronic Case Files) and E-JUROR (electronic juror reporting) systems may be unavailable due to routine maintenance and upgrades. Jurors are encouraged to call 1-800-524-9609 for their reporting information if the E-JUROR system is unavailable during this time.
