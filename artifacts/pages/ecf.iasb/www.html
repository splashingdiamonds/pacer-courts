Welcome

Welcome to the official website for the United States Bankruptcy Court for the Southern District of Iowa.

The United States Bankruptcy Court for the Southern District of Iowa will be closed on the following dates in observance of the Christmas and New Year holidays: Friday, December 23, Monday, December 26, Friday, December 30 and Monday, January 2.
Electronic filing will be available for use during the referenced time frame.
