Welcome

Welcome to the official website for the United States Bankruptcy Court for the Southern District of Ohio. We have offices in Cincinnati, Columbus, and Dayton.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to uphold the integrity of the judicial process by accurately maintaining the records of the Court and providing exceptional service to those seeking justice through the bankruptcy system.

We are committed to treating all individuals, whether experiencing financial difficulties or protecting their interest, with fairness, respect, and professionalism.
