NOTICE:

COVID-19: The United States District Court for the Southern District of Indiana's Response to Coronavirus Disease (COVID-19). NOTE: Anyone entering a federal courthouse in the Southern District of Indiana will be asked at the security station, "Regardless of your vaccination status, have you experienced any symptoms of COVID-19 in the last 48 hours?" If you develop COVID-19 within the 14 days after your visit to one of the Southern District of Indiana's courthouses, please call Emergency Preparedness Coordinator Doria Lynch at 317-229-3729 or email at doria_lynch@insd.uscourts.gov.
Attention Pro Se Filers: Filings are currently being accepted by drop box, email, or in-person. For more information, please click HERE.
Criminal Debt Payments: The Clerk's Office does not accept credit card payments over the phone, in person, or through Pay.Gov for any type of Criminal Debt payment. Please mail a check or money order made payable to Clerk, United States District Court, 46 East Ohio Street, Room 105, Indianapolis, IN 46204.
