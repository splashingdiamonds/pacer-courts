CM/ECF and PACER

NextGen CM/ECF

We have completed upgrading our CM/ECF system to NextGen CM/ECF. For more information, go to NextGen Information.

Welcome to Alabama Southern District Court's Electronic Case Filing system (CM/ECF). Counsel must file all documents electronically through CM/ECF. Additionally, all counsel must register for electronic service of documents. Attorney Admission and CM/ECF Registration. 

There are certain fees for using the Public Access to Court Electronic Records (PACER) system. Review the Electronic Public Access Fee Schedule for PACER by clicking here.
