February 19, 2020, CM/ECF Dictionary Changes

In connection with the Small Business Reorganization Act of 2019 that takes effect on February 19, 2020, the United States Bankruptcy Court for the Northern District of Indiana will add the following events to the CM/ECF dictionary:

  • Bankruptcy > Plan > Chapter 11 Small Business Subchapter V Plan
  • Bankruptcy > Other > Pre-Status Conference Report
  • Bankruptcy > Trustee/US Trustee > Notice Appointing Subchapter V Trustee
  • Bankruptcy > Notice > Notice of Substantial Consummation
  • Bankruptcy > Trustee/US Trustee > Ch. 11 Subch V Case Trustees Report of NoDistribution (various versions A-F)
  • Bankruptcy > Trustee/US Trustee > Chapter 11 Subchapter V Trustees Final Report and Account

April 24, 2020, CM/ECF Dictionary Change

In connection with the Coronavirus Aid, Relief, and Economic Security Act that became effective March 27, 2020, the United States Bankruptcy Court for the Northern District of Indiana has added the following event to the CM/ECF dictionary:

  • Bankruptcy > Claim Actions or Creditor Claim Actions or Notices > Notice of Mortgage Forbearance

May 27, 2021, CM/ECF Dictionary Change

In connection with the Consolidated Appropriations Act that became effective December 27, 2020, the United States Bankruptcy Court for the Northern District of Indiana has added the following event to the CM/ECF dictionary:

  • Bankruptcy > Motions/Applications > Discharge Pursuant to Section 1328(i)

June 21, 2021, CM/ECF Dictionary Change

In connection with two new forms prescribed by the Department of Justice’s U.S. Trustee Program for chapter 11 periodic reports in non-small business chapter 11 cases, the United States Bankruptcy Court for the Northern District of Indiana has added the following events to the CM/ECF dictionary:

  • Chapter 11 Monthly Operating Report
  • Chapter 11 Post Confirmation Report
  • Small Business Operating Report
