Coronavirus Announcement

UPDATED September 12, 2022

Masks are optional in public areas of the Potter Stewart U.S. Courthouse in Cincinnati, the Joseph P. Kinneary U.S. Courthouse in Columbus, and the Walter H. Rice Federal Building and U.S. Courthouse in Dayton.

 Judicial Officers and Court Unit Executives may implement additional safety protocols, including requiring masks, in their respective offices, chambers, and courtrooms.
