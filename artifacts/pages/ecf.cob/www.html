Notices

Court Closed 12/26 and 1/2

The Court will be closed Monday, December 26 for the Christmas holiday and Monday January 2, 2023 for the New Years holiday. Please plan your filings accordingly. For emergencies requiring immediate action on Friday, November 25, please call 720-904-7303.

CM/ECF System Maintenance 12/15-12/19

The Court's electronic filing system will be down for maintenance on Thursday, December 15 at 5:00 p.m. through Monday, December 19 at 8:00 a.m., Mountain time. Please plan your filings accordingly. For emergency filings, please contact the Clerk’s Office at 720-904-7300. Thank you.

COVID-19 Updates and Information

If Denver County’s level of community transmission is rated “High,” then masks are required in common areas of the courthouse. Please visit our COVID-19 Information page for more detail.
