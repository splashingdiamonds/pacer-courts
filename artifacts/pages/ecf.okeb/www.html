Welcome

Welcome to the official website for the United States Bankruptcy Court for the Eastern District of Oklahoma located in Muskogee, OK.   MORE »

HOURS: 8:30 AM - 4:30 PM

Attorneys, Creditors with filing accounts, and Trustees are required to file all pleadings and payments through CM/ECF. Trustees may submit minimal or registry fund payments using the pay.gov on-line payment form. Debtors and Creditors should make all fee payments using the pay.gov on-line payment form. New bankruptcy cases filed without attorney representation should be filed online using eSR  Electronic Self Representation under the red tab titled Filing Without an Attorney.  Proofs of Claims should be filed on-line using ePOC Electronic Proof of claim under the red tab titled For Creditors. If you have an emergency filing or need assistance filing on-line, please call the Clerk's Office at the number listed below.

PHONE: (918)549-7200 Click here for DIRECTIONS »
