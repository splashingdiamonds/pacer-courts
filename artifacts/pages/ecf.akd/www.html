CM/ECF Info

Questions

Any party filing a pleading or other document electronically that is time sensitive must immdiately contact the Clerk's Office at 907-677-6130 and provide them with the following information:

  • Case number
  • Docket number

Regarding filings etc. which do not require immediate attention, may also be sent to cmecfhelpdesk@akd.uscourts.gov.

    CM/ECF for the District of Alaska Website: https://ecf.akd.uscourts.gov
