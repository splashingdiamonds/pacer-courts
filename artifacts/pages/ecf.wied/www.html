  • Milwaukee Division

    Counties Served

    Counties served include Dodge, Fond du Lac, Green Lake, Kenosha, Marquette, Milwaukee, Ozaukee, Racine, Sheboygan, Walworth, Washington, and Waukesha

  • Green Bay Division

    Counties served include Brown, Calumet, Door, Florence, Forest, Kewaunee, Langlade, Manitowoc, Marinette, Menominee, Oconto, Outagamie, Shawano, Waupaca, Waushara, and Winnebago

  • Milwaukee Visitor Information

    Situated on the shores of Lake Michigan, Milwaukee is known as a great place by a great lake.  It is home to the Milwaukee Brewers, the Milwaukee Bucks, the Milwaukee Admirals and the Milwaukee Wave. 

  • Green Bay Visitor Information

    Located at the head of Green Bay, the city of Green Bay is the smallest city to host a professional sports team.  Home to the Green Bay Packers, with 13 NFL championships, the city is also known as Titletown, USA. It really is "Better by the Bay".
