UPDATE REGARDING HEARINGS

In-person courtroom appearances are now allowed. If a hearing has been scheduled by video conference or telephone conference, a party wishing to appear in person is required to notice other hearing participants of their intention to be in the courtroom. Please note that the court has not installed plexiglass or other Covid-19 safety measures; however, participants are encouraged to wear masks and maintain social distancing.
