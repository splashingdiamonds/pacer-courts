Welcome

Welcome to the official website for the United States District Court for the Northern District of West Virginia. We have four points of holding court located in Clarksburg, Elkins, Martinsburg and Wheeling. 

COURT OPERATIONS AND COVID-19

The Court remains open and is conducting necessary judicial business, is accepting filings and responding to inquiries. Click here to view orders and information pertaining to court operations. 

Next Generation of CM/ECF (NextGen) is Here!

The United States District Court for the Northern District of West Virginia (WVND) upgraded to the Next Generation of CM/ECF (NextGen) on July 19, 2021.  All CM/ECF users must act and take specific steps to continue to file electronically with WVND on or after July 19, 2021.  Please click here for NextGen Resources and/Information.
