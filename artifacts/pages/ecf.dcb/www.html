  • Mission Statement

    Our mission at the Clerk's Office is to fulfill the judicial expectations of the public by providing exceptional service, while honorably upholding the administration of justice.

  • Bankruptcy Assistance Center

    Need answers about Bankruptcy? Visit the Bankruptcy Assistance Center on the 1st and 3rd Friday of the month.

  • John Marshall Park

    Neighboring the E. Barrett Prettyman Courthouse is John Marshall Park. The park was named in honor of John Marshall, Chief Justice of the Supreme Court from 1801-1835, who at one time resided at the park's location.
